4-7-8 Breathing Technique
  1. Sit comfortably and place the tip of your tongue against the ridge behind your upper front teeth.
  2. Exhale completely through your mouth, making a whoosh sound.
  3. Close your mouth and inhale quietly through your nose to a count of 4.
  4. Hold your breath for a count of 7.
  5. Exhale completely through your mouth to a count of 8, making a whoosh sound.

üßò Mindfulness Meditation

Practicing mindfulness can reduce stress, improve focus, and enhance emotional regulation.

5-Minute Body Scan
  1. Find a comfortable position either sitting or lying down.
  2. Close your eyes and take a few deep breaths.
  3. Bring your attention to your feet. Notice any sensations without judgment.
  4. Slowly move your attention up through your body - legs, hips, abdomen, chest, arms, neck, and head.
  5. If your mind wanders, gently bring your focus back to the body part you're scanning.
  6. When you reach the top of your head, take a moment to notice how your entire body feels.

Gratitude Journaling
